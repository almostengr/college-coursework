About Me

My name is MS. I am currently a sophomore at the University of Alabama working on a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration majoring in Management Information Systems with a minor in Computer Science. I am scheduled to graduate in May, 2011 and hope to obtain a job at an accounting firm.

I am from the small town of Hartselle in north Alabama. I am living out my childhood dream attending the University of Alabama and loving it! In my free time, I enjoy reading, spending time with my freinds and being outdoors.  When I get the change, I love returning home to my small town and spending time with my family and high school friends.  Being away from my family gets hard, however they support me with everything I do. I know that no matter what I decide to do in my life, they will be behind me 100%.
