About Me

Hello!! My name is AM. I was born in Selma, Alabama.  At the age of four, I moved to Birmingham, Alabama where I graduated from Clay-Chalkville High School.  I am currently a sophomore at The University of Alabama in Tuscaloosa, where I am pursuing my Bachelor of Science in Business Administration majoring in Management Information Systems with a minor in Computer Science.  When I graduate, I want to pursue my career at a consulting firm where I am able to travel and further my education.   I am also currently a Leasing Agent at Campus Way Apartments and I love my job because it helps me interact with different clients, students, and future residents.  My interests include shopping, traveling, and volunteering at the Boys and Girls Club in Tuscaloosa, Alabama where I help mentor younger kids.  Trustworthy and determind is how I would describe myself.  Dedication is the key to success, so I work hard to accomplish all goals that I want to achieve.
