Located along the banks of the Black Warrior River in west-central Alabama, Tuscaloosa is led by a Mayor/Council form of government and is home to approximately 89,000 residents as well as a thriving business community. In the words of Mayor Walter Maddox, we welcome you to absolutely the best city in the nation and one of its most progressive communities.
Source: City of Tuscaloosa Website
